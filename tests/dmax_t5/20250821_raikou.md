## Summary

Raikou has received a HP buff since [last time](./20250315_raikou.md). Raikou has the same CPM of `0.8`, but it got an HP increase from `20k` to `25k`:

Stats     | Values
:-------- | :------
HP        | `25k`
CPM       | `0.8`
*AtkMult* | `2.0`
*DefMult* | `1.0`

*DefCPM*  | `0.8`
