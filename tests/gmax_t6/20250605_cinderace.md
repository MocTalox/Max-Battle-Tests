# Cinderace

Cinderace was available in T6 Gigantamax Battles from June 6th to June 8th in-person on Jersey City during the *Jersey City, United States - Pokémon GO Fest 2025* event. Later it became available on June 7th and 8th globaly during the *Instrumental Wonders* event, from June 23rd to June 27th globaly during the *Ancients Recovered* event and on June 28th and 29th globaly during the *Pokémon GO Fest 2025: Global* event. The stats remained the same during all events.
