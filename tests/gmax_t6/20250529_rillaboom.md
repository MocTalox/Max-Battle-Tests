# Rillaboom

Rillaboom was available in T6 Gigantamax Battles from May 29th to June 1st in-person on Osaka during the *Osaka, Japan - Pokémon GO Fest 2025* event. Later it became available on May 31st and June 1st globaly during the *Serene Retreat* event, from June 23rd to June 27th globaly during the *Ancients Recovered* event and on June 28th and 29th globaly during the *Pokémon GO Fest 2025: Global* event. The stats remained the same during all events.
