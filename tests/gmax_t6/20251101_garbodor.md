# Garbodor

Garbodor was available in T6 Gigantamax Battles on November 1st globaly during the *Gigantamax Garbodor Max Battle Day* event.

## Summary

Garbodor has an Attack CPM value in the range `1.260000` - `1.260009`, and very likely a HP value of `100k` and a Defense CPM value of `1.4`, which means it has a CPM of `1.4` and an Attack Multiplier of `0.9`:

Stats     | Values
:-------- | :------
HP        | `100k`
CPM       | `1.4`
*AtkMult* | `0.9`
*DefMult* | `1.0`
*AtkCPM*  | `1.26`
*DefCPM*  | `1.4`
