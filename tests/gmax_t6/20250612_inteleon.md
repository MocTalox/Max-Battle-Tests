# Inteleon

Inteleon was available in T6 Gigantamax Battles from June 13rd to June 15th in-person on Paris during the *Paris, France - Pokémon GO Fest 2025* event. Later it became available on June 14th and 15th globaly during the *Phantom Ruins* event, from June 23rd to June 27th globaly during the *Ancients Recovered* event and on June 28th and 29th globaly during the *Pokémon GO Fest 2025: Global* event. The stats remained the same during all events.
