# Lapras

Lapras was available in T6 Gigantamax Battles on July 19th globaly during the *Gigantamax Lapras Max Battle Day* event.

## Summary

Lapras has received a massive nerf to its overall stats. Lapras has an Attack CPM value close to `0.34`, a HP value of `100k` and a Defense CPM value in the range `0.433003` - `0.456457`, which means it has a CPM of `0.45` and an Attack Multiplier of `0.75`:

Stats     | Previous | Current
:-------- | :------- | :-------
HP        | `80k`    | `100k`
CPM       | `0.85`   | `0.45`
*AtkMult* | `0.9`    | `0.75`
*DefMult* | `1.0`    | `1.0`
*AtkCPM*  | `0.765`  | `0.3375`
*DefCPM*  | `0.85`   | `0.45`
