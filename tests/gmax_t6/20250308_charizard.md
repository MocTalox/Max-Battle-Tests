# Charizard

Charizard was available in T6 Gigantamax Battles on March 8th and 9th globaly during the *Powerful Potential* event.

## Summary

Charizard has received a slight nerf to its Attack Multiplier since [last time](./20241026_charizard.md). Charizard has an Attack CPM value in the range `0.765` - `0.765`, a HP value of `80k` and a Defense CPM value in the range `0.85` - `0.85`, which means it has the same CPM and an Attack Multiplier of `0.9`.

Stats     | Previous | Current
:-------- | :------- | :-------
HP        | `80k`    | `80k`
CPM       | `0.85`   | `0.85`
*AtkMult* | `1.0`    | `0.9`
*DefMult* | `1.0`    | `1.0`
*AtkCPM*  | `0.85`   | `0.765`
*DefCPM*  | `0.85`   | `0.85`
